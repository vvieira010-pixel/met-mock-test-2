MET Mock Test 2 is a fresh practice exam designed to help you build on your preparation. With new questions and prompts across all sections, this test follows the same format and difficulty as the official Michigan English Test (MET).

How to Ace This Test

  1. 1
    Build on your practice. If you took Mock Test 1, focus on the sections you found hardest. If this is your first test, start with Writing and go in order.
  2. 2
    Watch your timing. Every section has a visible timer. Use it to pace yourself — each minute counts.
  3. 3
    Review your answers. Check your results after each section to understand your CEFR level and track your progress.
